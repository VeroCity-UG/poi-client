XML3D GeoVis Bundle Demo
3D-Map - Oulu
  • GIS Data Provider GE for aerial imaging and buildings
  • 3D-UI GE (xml3d)
  • POI Data Provider GE for Points of Interest
  • 3D-Map Tiles SE for buildings
How To:
The demo above shows the Finnish city of Oulu, together with a selected number of POIs. Please feel free to have a look at the provided script files to understand how you can create your own applications with this bundle. For a starter, you may try to:
  • Specify the POI and GIS backends of your bundle in the config.js file
  • Select the region you would like to display by changing the long and lat values in buildings_oulu.html
